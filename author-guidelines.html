作者指南Author Guidelines

本页说明《易經與文明》的投稿范围、编辑与同行评审流程、稿件体例、学术伦理、版权与开放获取政策，供作者正式投稿时参照。 This page outlines the submission scope, editorial and peer-review process, manuscript requirements, ethics standards, copyright terms, and open-access policy of I CHING AND CIVILIZATION for formal author reference.

ChecklistChecklist

投稿前请确认Before You Submit

  • 稿件须为原创成果，且不得同时投往其他期刊或出版渠道。Submissions must be original and may not be under consideration elsewhere.
  • 研究论文请控制在 10,000 字以内，并附 100-150 字摘要。Research articles should not exceed 10,000 words and must include a 100-150 word abstract.
  • 为便于双盲评审，请勿在稿件正文中出现作者姓名或其他身份信息。To support double-blind review, the author’s name and identifying details should not appear in the manuscript.
  • 如附图版、表格或引文材料，请同时说明来源、版权与授权状态。If you include figures, tables, or source materials, provide full source and permissions information.
TypesTypes

可接收的稿件类型Accepted Submission Types

《易經與文明》欢迎围绕《周易》文本、经学传统、思想史、比较文明、跨语际译介与接受史展开的来稿，也接受与本刊宗旨密切相关的书评、综述与精选学术动态。 I CHING AND CIVILIZATION welcomes submissions on Zhouyi texts, classical traditions, intellectual history, comparative civilization, translation, and reception history, as well as book reviews, review essays, and selected scholarly reports related to the journal’s mission.

Type I

研究论文Research Article

适用于围绕《周易》文献、经学传统、哲学、神学、思想史与文明比较展开的完整研究论文。For full-length scholarly articles on Zhouyi texts, classical traditions, philosophy, theology, intellectual history, and comparative civilization.

Type II

文本札记Textual Note

适合围绕异文、出土文献、训诂问题、图像材料或局部史料发现展开的专门讨论。For focused discussions of variants, excavated texts, philological questions, images, and closely argued source discoveries.

Type III

译介与评注Translation and Commentary

适用于海外重要论文、历史文献或经典解释文本的译介，并需附必要的学术导言与注释。For translations of important essays, historical documents, or interpretive texts, accompanied by a scholarly introduction and commentary.

Type IV

书评或综述Book Review or Review Essay

适用于围绕新近出版著作、专题研究趋势或学术争点展开的评论、综述与评述。For critical reviews of recent books, review essays, and synthetic assessments of current debates in the field.

Type V

学术动态Scholarly Report

可酌情接收与本刊方向相关的会议述评、资料整理、项目综述与专题动态。Selected conference reports, source surveys, project overviews, and thematic scholarly updates may also be considered.

PoliciesPolicies

投稿政策与审稿流程Submission Policies and Review Process

作者在使用投稿入口前，应先阅读下列正式要求。它们构成《易經與文明》当前的编辑与出版基本政策。 Authors should read the following requirements before using the submission interface. They set out the journal’s current editorial and publishing policies.

SubmissionSubmission

投稿要求Submission Requirements

  • 研究论文、文本札记、译介与评注、书评及综述，均应通过本页面启动投稿。Research articles, textual notes, translations, commentaries, and reviews should all be initiated through this portal.
  • 本刊不收取投稿费，录用后亦不支付稿酬。The journal charges no submission fee and does not offer monetary remuneration for accepted work.
  • 来稿不得同时投往其他期刊或已在其他出版物上发表。Submissions must not be under consideration elsewhere or previously published.
ReviewReview

编辑与同行评审Editorial and Peer Review Process

  • 本刊通常采用双盲同行评审制度评估研究论文与适合外审的学术稿件。The journal typically uses a double-blind peer-review process for research articles and other suitable scholarly submissions.
  • 编辑部先进行初步审核，再将适合的稿件送交外审。After an initial editorial audit, suitable submissions are sent out for review.
  • 评审周期通常为 3-6 个月，复杂稿件有时可能更久。The review process typically takes 3-6 months, and sometimes longer for complex submissions.
ManuscriptManuscript

稿件要求Manuscript Requirements

  • 本刊欢迎经学、哲学、神学、思想史及相关领域的专业学者、青年学者与博士研究生投稿。The editors welcome submissions from professional scholars, early-career researchers, and doctoral candidates in classics, philosophy, theology, intellectual history, and related fields.
  • 为便于匿名评审，稿件正文不应出现作者姓名与明显身份信息。To facilitate blind review, the author’s name should not appear in the manuscript itself.
  • 研究论文一般不超过 10,000 字（含注释），并须附 100-150 字摘要。Research articles should normally be no longer than 10,000 words, including notes, and must include a 100-150 word abstract.
  • 文本札记、译介与评注可根据材料性质适度短于研究论文。Textual notes, translations, and commentaries may be shorter when appropriate to the material.
Review EssayReview Essay

书评要求Book Review Requirements

  • 书评或综述以 750-1000 字为宜，少于 500 字或多于 1500 字通常不予采用。Book reviews should normally be 750-1000 words and are generally not accepted if fewer than 500 or more than 1500 words.
  • 书评须注明作者、书名、出版社及出版年份。All reviews must identify the author, title, publisher, and year of publication.
  • 评论应说明该书在当前学术语境中的位置与贡献。Reviews should situate the work within current scholarship and explain its contribution.
StyleStyle

体例与引注Style and Documentation

  • 本刊采用《芝加哥格式手册》作为基本体例依据。The journal follows the Chicago Manual of Style as its basic style guide.
  • 引注可使用尾注体例，也可采用作者-年份格式，但须全文统一。Contributors may use either endnotes or the author-date system, provided the format is consistent throughout.
  • 建议提供中英文摘要与关键词，便于双语展示与后续检索。Chinese and English abstracts and keywords are strongly encouraged for bilingual presentation and indexing.
  • 本刊偏重文本、思想与诠释研究，强量化稿件宜另投更适合的期刊；图表与示意图应尽量从简。Given the journal’s textual and interpretive focus, strongly quantitative work may be better suited elsewhere, and figures, charts, and diagrams should be kept to a minimum.
EthicsEthics

学术伦理Ethics Policy

  • 来稿必须杜绝抄袭、数据伪造与事实篡改。Submissions must be free of plagiarism, data fabrication, and falsification.
  • 稿件评估仅依据其学术内容、原创性与学术相关性，不以作者的种族、性别、宗教、国籍或政治立场为判断依据。Manuscripts are evaluated solely on intellectual content, originality, and scholarly relevance, without regard to the author’s race, gender, religion, citizenship, or political philosophy.
  • 编辑、作者与审稿人应主动披露利益冲突；如已刊文章不符合上述标准，编辑部保留撤稿权。Editors, authors, and reviewers must disclose conflicts of interest. Retractions may be issued if published work is found not to meet these standards.
CopyrightCopyright

版权与转载Copyright

  • 作者投稿即保证作品为原创成果，且不侵犯任何既有版权或第三方权利。Authors warrant that submitted work is original and does not infringe any existing copyright or third-party rights.
  • 除非另有约定，稿件一经录用，相关出版版权由出版方持有；作者通常可在申请后获得转载许可。Unless otherwise agreed, legal copyright in accepted work is held by the publisher, and reprint rights are normally granted upon request.
  • 为便于传播，出版方可授权第三方数据库或平台收录、展示与分发文章，作者不因此另获报酬。For dissemination, the publisher may authorize third-party databases and platforms to reproduce, incorporate, display, and distribute the work, without additional compensation to the author.
Open AccessOpen Access

开放获取政策Open Access Policy

  • 本刊坚持即时开放获取，已刊内容原则上向公众免费开放。The journal provides immediate open access to its published content.
  • 我们相信，让研究成果被自由阅读、引用与传播，有助于推动全球范围内的知识交流。We believe that making research freely available supports a broader global exchange of knowledge.

  • 请在弹出的邮件客户端中手动附加匿名稿件、作者信息页及必要附件。Attach the anonymized manuscript, author information page, and any required supplementary files in the email client that opens.
  • 研究论文建议附中英文摘要、关键词及规范化参考文献。Research articles should ideally include Chinese and English abstracts, keywords, and standardized references.
