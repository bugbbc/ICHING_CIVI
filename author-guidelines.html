作者指南 Author Guidelines

GuidelinesGuidelines

正式作者指南Author Guidelines

下列 12 项构成《易经与文明》当前适用的正式作者指南。投稿前请完整阅读，以确保稿件、附件与邮件信息符合要求。 The following twelve sections constitute the current formal guidelines for contributors to the Journal of I Ching and Civilization. Please read them carefully before submission to ensure that the manuscript and supporting files comply with journal requirements.

01

投稿方式Submission Process

  • 本刊采用电子邮件投稿。请将稿件发送至：submission@ichingandcivilization.orgManuscripts should be submitted by email to: submission@ichingandcivilization.org
  • 投稿时须提交论文正文（匿名处理，用于同行评审）以及作者信息单独文件。Authors must include a complete anonymous manuscript file for peer review and a separate file containing author information.
  • 作者信息文件应包括：姓名、工作单位、联系邮箱、简要学术简介（50–100字）。The separate author-information file should include the full name, institutional affiliation, email address, and a brief biographical note (50–100 words).
  • 邮件标题格式：投稿—作者姓名—论文标题。The email subject line should follow the format: Submission – Author Name – Article Title.
  • 投稿后，作者将收到确认邮件。稿件格式须为 Word（.doc 或 .docx）。Authors will receive an acknowledgment email upon submission. Submissions must be in Word format (.doc or .docx).
02

基本要求General Requirements

  • 稿件须为原创成果，未发表且未同时投稿他刊。Submissions must be original, unpublished, and not under consideration elsewhere.
  • 本刊接受中文与英文稿件。The journal accepts English and Chinese manuscripts.
  • 所有投稿实行双盲同行评审制度。All submissions undergo double-blind peer review.
  • 稿件正文中不得出现作者身份信息。Manuscripts must not contain identifying information.
03

稿件类型与篇幅Manuscript Types and Length

  • 研究论文：5000–10000字。Research Articles: 5,000–10,000 words.
  • 综述论文：3000–6000字。Review Articles: 3,000–6,000 words.
  • 书评：800–1500字。Book Reviews: 800–1,500 words.
04

双语要求Language Policy (Bilingual Requirement)

  • 本刊实行双语发表制度。The journal adopts a bilingual publication model.
  • 稿件可用中文或英文投稿。Submissions may be in English or Chinese.
  • 录用后须提供：中英文标题、中英文摘要（各150–250字）、中英文关键词（3–6个）。Accepted articles must include an English and Chinese title, English and Chinese abstracts (150–250 words each), and 3–6 keywords in both languages.
05

稿件格式Manuscript Preparation

  • 稿件应使用双倍行距、标准页边距。Manuscripts should be double-spaced with standard margins.
  • 稿件应包含以下结构：标题、摘要、关键词、正文、参考文献。Manuscripts should include the following sections: Title, Abstract, Keywords, Main Text, and References.
  • 标题层级应简明，不编号。Use clear, concise, unnumbered headings.
  • 注释与引用采用芝加哥格式（Chicago Style）。Follow the Chicago Manual of Style for citations and notes.
06

图表要求Figures and Tables

  • 图表应尽量精简。Use figures and tables sparingly.
  • 请提供高清格式文件（TIFF、JPG、PNG）。Submit high-resolution files (TIFF, JPG, PNG).
  • 图表必须附标题说明并注明来源。Provide captions and sources for all figures and tables.
07

作者信息Author Information

  • 作者信息须单独提交，以确保匿名评审。Author details must be submitted separately to ensure anonymity.
  • 应包括：姓名、单位、邮箱、简要学术简介。The file should include the full name, institutional affiliation, contact email, and a short biography.
08

审稿流程Peer Review Process

  • 编辑初审。Initial editorial screening.
  • 双盲同行评议（通常 2 位审稿人）。Double-blind peer review, typically involving two reviewers.
  • 审稿周期约 2–4 个月。The review period is approximately 2–4 months.
09

学术规范Publication Ethics

  • 作者须保证论文原创性。Authors must ensure the originality of their work.
  • 文献引用须规范准确。Proper citation of sources is required.
  • 严禁抄袭或任何学术不端行为。No plagiarism or academic misconduct is permitted.
  • 严禁一稿多投。Simultaneous submission to other journals is not permitted.
10

开放获取与版权Open Access and Copyright

  • 本刊为开放获取期刊。This journal is fully open access.
  • 作者保留版权。Authors retain copyright.
  • 文章采用 CC BY 4.0 许可协议发表。Articles are published under the CC BY 4.0 License.
11

费用说明Fees

  • 不收取投稿费。No submission fees.
  • 不收取版面费（APC）。No article processing charges (APC).
12

免责声明Disclaimer

  • 本刊所发表文章仅代表作者个人观点，不代表编辑部或出版机构立场。The views expressed in published articles are those of the authors and do not necessarily reflect those of the editorial board or publisher.
