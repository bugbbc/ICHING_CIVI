编辑委员会Editorial Board

本页公布《易經與文明》的编委架构、栏目分工与评审流程，说明期刊在学术方向、编辑协调、匿名评审与国际交流方面的组织方式。 This page presents the journal’s editorial structure, section responsibilities, and review procedures, outlining how I CHING AND CIVILIZATION organizes intellectual direction, editorial coordination, blind review, and international exchange.

编委会页面按照主编、执行编辑、栏目编辑与国际顾问等岗位组织，便于持续公布正式任命信息与学术分工。 The board page is organized around the editor-in-chief, managing editor, section editors, and international advisors so that formal appointments and scholarly responsibilities may be published clearly.

编辑委员会配置Editorial Configuration

  • 主编负责学术方向、专题设置与终审判断。The editor-in-chief defines editorial direction, dossiers, and final acceptance decisions.
  • 执行编辑负责流程协调、稿件管理与出版节奏。The managing editor coordinates workflow, manuscript handling, and production timing.
  • 栏目编辑按研究领域处理来稿并组织匿名评审。Section editors assign reviewers and handle manuscripts by field.
  • 国际顾问板块强化期刊的跨语际与跨文明接口。An international advisory circle strengthens the journal's cross-linguistic and civilizational reach.

核心编辑岗位Core Editorial Roles

本刊编委体系围绕学术方向、编辑协调、文本研究、比较研究、译介工作与国际顾问支持而设置，体现期刊的基本治理结构。 The editorial structure is arranged around intellectual direction, editorial coordination, textual studies, comparative work, translation, and advisory support, reflecting the journal’s core governance model.

主编席位Editor-in-Chief

学术方向Editorial Direction

统筹期刊宗旨、栏目边界、专题策划、终审标准与整体学术气质。Oversees mission, section boundaries, dossier planning, final review standards, and the overall intellectual character of the journal.

执行编辑Managing Editor

流程与出版协调Workflow and Production

负责收稿节奏、匿名送审、修回联络、版面准备与卷期发布统筹。Handles intake, blind review coordination, revisions, production preparation, and issue scheduling.

文本研究编辑Textual Studies Editor

版本与材料板块Texts and Materials

负责《周易》版本学、简帛文献、异文系统与材料整理类稿件。Oversees manuscripts on textual criticism, bamboo and silk manuscripts, variants, and documentary foundations.

比较研究编辑Comparative Editor

经典与文明比较Classics and Civilizations

统筹经典互证、思想史、文明比较与跨学科研究类来稿。Coordinates work on classical comparison, intellectual history, civilizational dialogue, and interdisciplinary research.

译介编辑Translation Editor

海外学界与译介专栏Global Reception and Translation

处理海外研究综述、译文、评注与国际接受史相关稿件。Handles translations, reception history, review essays, and work engaging overseas scholarship.

学术顾问Advisory Circle

国际与跨领域接口International and Cross-Field Reach

构成期刊的外部学术支持网络，增强国际能见度、跨语际对话与议题广度。Provides external scholarly support for the journal, strengthening international visibility, cross-linguistic dialogue, and thematic breadth.

审稿与编务流程Review and Editorial Workflow

下列流程说明本刊对来稿进行初审、外审、终审与排期发布的基本编辑程序。 The workflow below outlines the journal’s basic editorial procedure for screening, external review, final decision, and issue scheduling.

01

初审分流Initial Screening

编辑部按栏目与主题对稿件做初步分类，判断是否进入外审。The editorial office screens submissions by field and section before external review.

02

匿名评审Blind Review

每篇文章原则上进入匿名同行评审，并根据意见进行录用、修回或退稿判断。Each article normally proceeds to blind peer review, leading to acceptance, revision, or rejection.

03

终审与排期Final Decision and Scheduling

终审后进入卷期排程、摘要校核、版式准备与网页发布。After final decision, manuscripts move into issue scheduling, abstract checks, page preparation, and web publication.
