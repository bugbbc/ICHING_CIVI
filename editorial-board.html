创刊期编委配置Founding Editorial Configuration

  • 主编负责学术方向、专题设置与终审判断。The editor-in-chief defines editorial direction, dossiers, and final acceptance decisions.
  • 执行编辑负责流程协调、稿件管理与出版节奏。The managing editor coordinates workflow, manuscript handling, and production timing.
  • 栏目编辑按研究领域处理来稿并组织匿名评审。Section editors assign reviewers and handle manuscripts by field.
  • 国际顾问板块强化期刊的跨语际与跨文明接口。An international advisory circle strengthens the journal's cross-linguistic and civilizational reach.

审稿与编务流程Review and Editorial Workflow

这个流程卡片可以作为后续投稿系统的数据逻辑参考，也可以直接转写到正式期刊管理平台里。 These workflow cards can later serve as the basis for your actual submission-management logic.

01

初审分流Initial Screening

编辑部按栏目与主题对稿件做初步分类，判断是否进入外审。The editorial office screens submissions by field and section before external review.

02

匿名评审Blind Review

每篇文章原则上进入匿名同行评审，并根据意见进行录用、修回或退稿判断。Each article normally proceeds to blind peer review, leading to acceptance, revision, or rejection.

03

终审与排期Final Decision and Scheduling

终审后进入卷期排程、摘要校核、版式准备与网页发布。After final decision, manuscripts move into issue scheduling, abstract checks, page preparation, and web publication.
