编辑委员会 Editorial Board

本页公布《易经与文明》的正式编委名单。编辑委员会负责维护本刊的学术方向、审稿标准与出版质量。 This page publishes the formal editorial appointments of the Journal of I Ching and Civilization. The editorial board is responsible for maintaining the journal’s scholarly direction, review standards, and publication quality.

本刊采取同行评议制度，编委会在稿件遴选、匿名评审组织与学术把关方面承担核心职责。 The journal operates through peer review, and the editorial board plays a central role in manuscript selection, blind review coordination, and academic oversight.
