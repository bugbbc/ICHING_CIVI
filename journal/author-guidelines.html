作者指南Author Guidelines

这一页面既提供投稿要求，也承担当前版本的投稿入口。作者填写表单后，网页会自动生成投稿邮件草稿，便于通过静态站方式先行运行；后续你可以把它替换成正式的投稿系统。 This page combines author instructions with the current submission interface. The form generates a draft submission email so the site can function immediately as a static deployment, while remaining ready for a future full submission system.

ChecklistChecklist

投稿前请确认Before You Submit

  • 稿件未同时投往其他期刊。The manuscript is not under consideration elsewhere.
  • 摘要、关键词和注释体例已整理完成。Abstract, keywords, and notes have been prepared.
  • 如有图片、表格或古文字材料，已说明来源与版权状态。Images, tables, and manuscript materials include source and rights information.
  • 匿名审稿版与作者信息版文件可分开提供。Anonymous-review and author-information versions can be provided separately if needed.
TypesTypes

可接收的稿件类型Accepted Submission Types

当前类型设置已经兼顾文本研究、译介与综述，后续如需加“专题稿”“书评专栏”“会议述评”等栏目，可以继续扩充。 The current categories cover textual studies, translation work, and review essays. Additional sections can be added later for dossiers, book forums, or conference reports.

Type I

研究论文Research Article

适用于围绕《周易》文献、经学思想、文明比较或接受史展开的完整研究论文。For full scholarly arguments on Zhouyi texts, classical thought, civilizational comparison, or reception history.

Type II

文本札记Textual Note

适合版本异文、出土材料、训诂问题、图像材料或局部材料发现的专门讨论。For focused discussions of variants, excavated texts, philological issues, images, and source discoveries.

Type III

译介与评注Translation and Commentary

适用于海外重要论文、历史文献或经典解释文本的译介与学术说明。For translations of important essays, historical documents, or interpretive texts with scholarly commentary.

FormatFormat

基本格式Basic Formatting

  • 正文建议使用 Word 或兼容格式提交，正文、注释、参考文献层级清晰。Submit in Word or a compatible format, with clear hierarchy across text, notes, and bibliography.
  • 建议附中英文摘要和关键词，便于后续双语展示与检索。Provide Chinese and English abstracts and keywords when possible.
  • 涉及古文字、简帛图版时，请单独附高清文件和出处说明。For manuscript images and paleographic materials, attach high-resolution files with source notes.
EthicsEthics

学术伦理Publication Ethics

  • 作者需确保稿件原创性，注明引用来源，不得一稿多投。Authors must ensure originality, provide proper citation, and avoid simultaneous submission.
  • 如论文接受资助、数据支持或图像授权，请在稿件中说明。Funding, data support, and image permissions should be disclosed in the manuscript.
  • 编辑部保留在匿名评审基础上要求补充材料或修改格式的权利。The editorial office may request additional materials or formatting revision during review.
