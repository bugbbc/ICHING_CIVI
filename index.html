期刊简介 About the Journal

《易经与文明》是一份同行评议学术期刊，致力于研究《周易》及其在文明发展中的思想意义。 Journal of I Ching and Civilization is a peer-reviewed academic journal dedicated to the study of the I Ching (Book of Changes) and its significance in the development of human civilization.

本刊关注《周易》的哲学、文本与制度维度，尤其重视其在法律、治理与社会秩序中的理论价值。 The journal promotes rigorous scholarly inquiry into the philosophical, textual, and institutional dimensions of the I Ching, with particular attention to its relevance for understanding law, governance, and social order.

期刊以“文明研究”为方法，将《周易》置于中国传统思想体系与世界文明视野之中加以考察。 It adopts a civilizational perspective, situating the I Ching within both the classical Chinese intellectual tradition and the broader context of world civilizations.

本刊鼓励跨学科研究，包括哲学、历史、法学、政治理论及文明比较研究等领域。 The journal encourages interdisciplinary research, including but not limited to philosophy, history, law, political theory, and comparative civilization studies.

同时，期刊重视与国际学界的对话，推动《周易》与西方思想传统之间的比较研究与理论交流。 In addition, it seeks to foster dialogue between different intellectual traditions by engaging with global scholarship, including comparative studies with Western philosophical and institutional thought.

本刊旨在通过对经典思想的深入研究，推动文明研究的深化，并探索其对当代制度与思想问题的启示。 Journal of I Ching and Civilization aims to contribute to the renewal of civilizational studies by exploring how classical wisdom can inform contemporary intellectual and institutional challenges.

本刊将《周易》不仅视为一部经典文本，也视为理解文明问题的活的思想资源。 The journal approaches the I Ching not merely as a classical text, but as a living intellectual resource for the study of civilization.

办刊宗旨与范围 Aim and Scope

本刊致力于从文本、哲学与文明维度研究《周易》，推动中国古典思想与其他世界文明之间的学术对话，特别关注法律、治理与制度发展问题。 This journal is dedicated to the study of the I Ching in its textual, philosophical, and civilizational dimensions. It seeks to promote dialogue between classical Chinese thought and other world civilizations, with particular attention to law, governance, and institutional development.
