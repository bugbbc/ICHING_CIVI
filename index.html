《易經與文明》 I CHING AND CIVILIZATION

《易經與文明》是一个面向《周易》文本研究、经典互证、文明比较、跨语际译介与学术交流的独立期刊网站。站点采用清晰的栏目结构，便于组织投稿、编务、当期内容与过刊归档。 I CHING AND CIVILIZATION is an independent journal site devoted to Zhouyi textual studies, classical comparison, civilizational dialogue, translation, and scholarly exchange. Its structure is organized to support submissions, editorial work, current contents, and archival browsing.

Scope Scope

期刊的核心研究范围 Core Editorial Scope

期刊将《周易》研究放在一个更大的文明框架中理解，既重视文献基础，也重视跨经典与跨文明的解释能力。 The journal places Zhouyi studies in a wider civilizational frame, giving equal weight to textual foundations and comparative interpretive work.

Area I

文本研究与材料整理 Textual Study and Source Work

欢迎围绕传世本、帛书、简牍、异文系统、图像资料与版本谱系的研究论文与材料报告。 We invite studies on received texts, silk manuscripts, bamboo slips, variant systems, visual materials, and textual genealogies.

Area II

经典互证与思想史 Classical Comparison and Intellectual History

鼓励《周易》与《尚书》《诗经》《黄帝内经》及礼乐、政治、医学传统的综合研究。 The journal encourages work linking the Zhouyi with the Shangshu, Shijing, Huangdi Neijing, and wider ritual, political, and medical traditions.

Area III

文明比较与全球接受史 Civilizational Comparison and Global Reception

期刊尤其重视自然法传统、分析心理学、海外汉学与翻译史中的《易》学讨论。 Special attention is given to natural law traditions, analytical psychology, global Sinology, and the translation history of the Yijing.

Standards Standards

期刊发布标准 Journal Publication Standards

本刊以同行评审、双语摘要、规范引注、正式目录发布与长期归档为基本标准，确保研究成果能够被稳定阅读、检索与引用。 The journal is structured around peer review, bilingual abstracts, standardized documentation, formal tables of contents, and long-term archiving so that published research remains stable, citable, and discoverable.
